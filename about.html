About me

Hi! My name is Cooper Drobnich and I am from Rochester, Michigan.


I am a user experience designer and researcher and current student at the University of Michigan. My passion is to create experiences and digital spaces that make users feel comfortable and at home. I am particularly fascinated by how complex social dynamics shape our use of technology, and how we can study and leverage these complexities to create the most ergonomic user interface possible. I use my experience doing research and studying Anthropology and UX Design to perfectly plan and realize spaces like these.


If you’d like to get to know me or talk to me about my work, please contact me below or at drobnich@umich.edu. I can’t wait to get to know you!
