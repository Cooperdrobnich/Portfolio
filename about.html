About me

Hi! My name is Cooper Drobnich and I am from Rochester, Michigan.


I am currently a junior studying User Experience and Design at the University of Michigan - School of Information. In my past experience I have worked as a user experience designer and researcher for Credibly. My passion lies in working to create digital spaces that are enjoyable, and makes users feel comfortable and at home. I am particularly interested in data-driven systems, and incorporating research methods that allow developers to cater to the complex social dynamics of user needs. I am also currently pursuing a minor in Spanish, and I hope to work toward creating inclusive experiences for the United State's ever increasing Spanish-speaking population.


If you would like to touch base with me or discuss my work, please contact me below or at drobnich@umich.edu. I would love to get to know you!
